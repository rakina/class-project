OUR STORY.

Aperture Fixtures was founded by Cave Johnson as a shower curtain manufacturer. The name "Aperture Fixtures" was chosen to make the curtains appear more hygienic.
After making billions in the shower curtain industry, Cave Johnson turned into science. We gather the best researchers in the world in the hope of advancing science, for the good all of us.

We are currently doing research and testing for our three main projects:

  • The Heimlich Counter-Maneuver, A reliable technique for interrupting the life-saving Heimlich Maneuver.

  • The Take-A-Wish Foundation, A charitable organization that will purchase wishes from the parents of terminally ill children and redistribute them to wish-deprived but otherwise healthy adults.

  • The 'Portal' project - As described by Johnson when it was first laid out: "Some kind of rip in the fabric of space... That would... Well, it'd be like, I don't know, something that would help with the shower curtains I guess. I haven't worked this idea out as much as the wish-taking one."
